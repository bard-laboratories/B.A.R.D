Key Streams

  • Robotics Sentry Programme — autonomous platforms to secure perimeters, corridors and bunker access.
  • X‑Ray Scatter Experiments — material characterisation under controlled exposure; results fed into design tables.
  • Chemical Testing — sealed‑bunker trials with redundant decontamination stages and air handling.
  • Advanced Research — special materials and power concepts associated with deep‑site discoveries.

Documentation & Controls

Shift logs, materials cards, robotics schematics and contamination reports were aggregated in a central archive for correlation and incident response.
